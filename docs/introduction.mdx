## What is OptionsFi?

OptionsFi is the **settlement and pricing layer for on-chain options** on Solana. We connect DeFi protocols to professional market makers via RFQs (Request for Quote), enabling institutional-grade options infrastructure for any vault strategy.

## How It Works

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Vault as Your Vault
    participant SDK as OptionsFi SDK
    participant Router as RFQ Router
    participant MM as Market Makers
    
    Vault->>SDK: createRFQ(params)
    SDK->>Router: Broadcast RFQ
    Router->>MM: Request quotes
    MM->>Router: Submit quotes
    Router->>SDK: Stream quotes
    SDK->>Vault: onQuote callback
    Vault->>SDK: executeOption(quoteId)
    SDK->>Vault: Transaction executed
```
